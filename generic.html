What is Skelcha Design

I have a broad interest in up to date styles. I pride myself on creating the unique next best thing. Having always had an interest in fashion, art and culture I like to think have a wide range of knowledge on artists, designers, artistic movements and more… my mother says I was born with a pencil in my hand and I always kept a keen interest in the world of media my whole life!

After studying my A-levels in Art, Photography, Philosophy and Ethics I consider myself to have a broad range of talent and knowledge which are a helpful combination of skills needed to cover the broad spectrum of design which can please all audiences. Whether you like Van Gogh or Banksy we’ve got you covered. As a young student I consider myself to be well researched and up to date on the new styles of the world, this in turn ensures that the work I produce is trendy but isn’t going to become yesterdays news. But also I can create designs that are classic and timeless. I am also interested in working on pre-existing projects, by this mean I can add a fresh new look to your brand. If you are looking to rebrand, Skelcha Design is here to help you!
